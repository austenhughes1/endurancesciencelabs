Review and adjust each phase before completing your analysis
The automatic detection makes a best guess at each phase, but it is not perfect -- especially in shorter or edited clips. Before completing your analysis, check each phase card and adjust the frame if needed. The right frame for each phase makes a real difference in the accuracy of your results.
1
Click any phase card to open a detailed view. Each card includes a plain-language description of exactly what to look for in that phase, with coaching notes specific to that moment of the gait cycle.
2
Drag the scrubber to find the best frame. Use the +/-1f and +/-5f buttons for precise, frame-by-frame control.
3
Click ↻ Analyze once you have found the right frame -- the overlay and results update immediately.
4
When all phases look right, click the Complete Analysis button that appears at the bottom to generate your full report.
