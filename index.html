StrideScope

Biomechanical analysis in minutes

No specialist equipment. No lab visit. A side-view video and a browser is all you need.

01
Upload your video

Drop any side-view running clip — treadmill or outdoor. MP4, MOV, or WebM. Your video never leaves your device.

02
7 phases detected

The tool scans your video and identifies 7 key gait phases: left and right foot strike, left and right toe-off, mid-stance, peak knee flex, and terminal swing.

03
Review and refine

Each phase shows a skeleton overlay with angle arcs. Scrub frame-by-frame to perfect any phase, with plain-language coaching guidance built in for each one.

04
Read your results

Trunk lean, knee angles, hip extension, arm swing, and foot strike position — compared across all 7 phases and graded against reference ranges from elite runners.

Our philosophy

"Most athletes train by feel. The best athletes train by understanding."

— Endurance Science Labs

Data without context is noise. Every number we surface comes with an explanation of what it means for your performance and how to act on it.

Physiology is individual. Reference ranges are starting points. The goal is to build a picture of your own body over time — not compare you to an abstract average.

Accessibility matters. Lab-grade analysis shouldn't require a lab. We're building tools that bring the science to any athlete, anywhere, at any level.
