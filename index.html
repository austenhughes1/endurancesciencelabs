StrideScope

Biomechanical analysis in minutes

No specialist equipment. No lab visit. Just a side-view video of you running and a browser.

01
Upload a video

Drop any side-view running clip — treadmill or outdoor. MP4, MOV, or WebM. Your video never leaves your device.

02
Automatic phase detection

The tool scans your video and identifies 7 key gait phases: left and right foot strike, left and right toe-off, mid-stance, peak knee flex, and terminal swing.

03
Review and refine

Each phase card shows the detected frame with skeleton overlay and angle arcs. Scrub frame-by-frame to perfect any phase, with phase-specific coaching guidance built in.

04
Read your angles

Trunk lean, knee flexion, hip extension, elbow angle, and foot strike offset — compared across all 7 phases in a single summary table.

Our philosophy

"Most athletes train by feel. The best athletes train by understanding."

— Endurance Science Labs

Data without context is noise. Every metric we surface comes with an explanation of what it means for your performance and how to act on it.

Physiology is individual. Reference ranges are starting points. The goal is to build a database of your own benchmarks over time.

Accessibility matters. Lab-grade analysis shouldn't require a lab. We're building tools that bring the science to any athlete, anywhere.
