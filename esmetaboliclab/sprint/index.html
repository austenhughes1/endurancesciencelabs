◈ Sprint VLamax Test

Find your glycolytic ceiling.

A 15-second all-out sprint, with a blood-lactate sample before and a few minutes after, tells us how fast your muscles can produce lactate at maximum effort — your VLamax. This is the first input the Lactate Step Test needs to build your full metabolic profile.
