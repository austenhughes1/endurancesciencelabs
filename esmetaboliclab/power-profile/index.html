▶ Power / Pace-only Profile

A metabolic estimate
without the needle.

Enter four of your max efforts — a 15-second sprint, plus 3-minute, 6-minute, and 12-minute all-outs — and we'll estimate your VLamax, VO₂max, and the full metabolic profile that follows from them.

⚠ This is an estimate, not a measurement.

VLamax here is back-derived from your sprint power via an empirical regression, not measured from blood lactate. Real VLamax varies a lot at any given sprint power — two cyclists with the same 15-second peak can have meaningfully different glycolytic ceilings. VO₂max is more defensible (sustained 6-minute max effort is a standard surrogate in the lab) but still rests on assumed gross efficiency or running economy.

If you can run the Sprint VLamax Test and the Lactate Step Test instead, do that — it's the route to trust. Use this when blood sampling isn't an option.
