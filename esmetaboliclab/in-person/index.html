What's included

Lactate meter, strips, lancets, alcohol prep, gauze, fan — you just bring yourself and your bike or running shoes.

15-second all-out sprint with pre- and post-sprint sampling, covering the early-peak window most home protocols miss.

Full 5-minute-stage step test on your bike or a treadmill — we handle the timing, sampling, and stage cues while you focus on the effort.

Your VO₂max, MLSS, LT1, Fatmax, fat-burning sweet spot, and training zones — explained in plain language with training implications.

What you walk away with

A full metabolic profile — the same picture INSCYD-style commercial tests cost coaches several hundred dollars to produce. Pinned to your actual physiology, not a population average.

◆ Race-pace targets
A number you can hold — for the half-marathon, the 70.3, the 1-hour TT — derived from where your lactate production meets your clearance.
◈ Custom training zones
Coggan 7-zone, Friel 7-zone, or Seiler 3-zone — anchored to your MLSS and LT1, ready to paste into your training app.
▶ Fueling in g/min
How many grams of carbs you burn at every intensity — so you know exactly what to eat on long runs, race day, and tempo sessions before you bonk.

Fatmax — the pace where you burn fat fastest. The foundation of every aerobic-base block, and the intensity you can hold for hours.
